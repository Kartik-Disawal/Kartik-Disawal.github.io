About Me

I'm a Computer Science graduate from IIT Goa with hands-on experience in machine learning research, backend engineering, and full-stack development.

At Cognam Technologies, I built scalable banking APIs, integrated event-driven microservices with Kafka, and handled ISO8583 message processing for ATM/POS transactions.

I interned at UNSW Sydney under Dr. Rohitash Chandra, evaluating GANs using dimensionality reduction techniques like PCA, t-SNE, and UMAP.

My research interests lie in adversarial machine learning — specifically the robustness of quantised neural networks — and applying ML to socioeconomic problems.
